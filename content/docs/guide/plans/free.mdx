# Free — $0/mo

The Free tier exists so you can monitor your single side-project VPS without paying anything. It's not a stripped-down trial — every probe and every alert path is identical to Pro.

---

## What you get

- **1 server**
- **All probes** — Docker, systemd, TCP ports, disk, memory, load
- **Telegram alerts** + recovery alerts
- **24-hour alert history**
- **60-second alert coalescing**
- **3-minute heartbeat-based offline detection**

That's the full v1 product, capped at one server.

---

## The 24h history limit

The `events` and `alerts` rows for Free-tier servers are pruned after 24 hours. You'll still get realtime Telegram messages — only the *historical lookup* is limited.

If you need to look back further than yesterday (incident review, auditing, "did this happen last week?"), upgrade to **Pro** for 30-day retention or **Team** for 90.

---

## What's not on Free

- **Multi-channel alerts** — Free is Telegram-only at v1. Email / Discord / Slack ship in Pro (Phase 2).
- **Hosted dashboard** — `app.stackpatrol.dev` is a Pro+ feature.
- **Public status page** — Pro+ only.
- **Custom check scripts** — Pro+ only.

If your single server outgrows Free, [upgrade to Pro](./pro) — same setup, more headroom.
