# Pro — $5/mo

The "I run a few servers and don't want this to be expensive" tier. Most StackPatrol users land here.

---

## What you get

Everything in [Free](./free), plus:

- **10 servers** (vs 1 on Free)
- **30-day alert history** (vs 24h on Free)
- **Hosted dashboard** at `app.stackpatrol.dev` *(Phase 2)*
- **Email / Discord / Slack alert channels** *(Phase 2)*
- **Public status page per workspace** *(Phase 2)*
- **Custom check scripts** *(Phase 2)*
- **Server-side enforcement** — the control plane upholds the limits, no client-side license keys

The CLI binary is identical to Free. Limits are enforced server-side.

---

## When Pro starts being worth it

You should be on Pro if any of:

- **You have more than 1 server.** Free is hard-capped at 1.
- **You want to look at alerts older than yesterday.** 24h vs 30 days.
- **You want email / Discord / Slack** alongside Telegram (Phase 2).
- **You want a web dashboard** with charts and an incident timeline (Phase 2).
- **You want to write custom check scripts** for app-level health checks (Phase 2).
- **You'd rather keep your monitoring bill at $5/mo than free** because the time-to-fix when something breaks is worth $5.

---

## Billing

- **Stripe-managed.** Card / SEPA / wallet.
- **No annual lock-in** at v1. Cancel anytime; access drops to Free at end of period.
- **Server-count downgrades** are graceful — exceeding the new limit doesn't break monitoring; it blocks new server registrations until you free up slots.

---

## What's NOT in Pro

- **Multi-user workspaces** — Pro is single-operator. If you need 2+ humans on the same workspace with different roles, [Team](./team) is the tier.
- **On-call rotation** — Team only.
- **SSO / SAML / audit logs** — Phase 4 enterprise.

If you're a small team, you probably want [Team](./team).
