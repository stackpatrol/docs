# Team — $20/mo

For small teams that need more than one human in the loop. Lands in **Phase 3** (Nov 2026 – Apr 2027) — the v1 ship is Free + Pro only.

---

## What you'll get

Everything in [Pro](./pro), plus:

- **50 servers** (vs 10 on Pro)
- **90-day alert history** (vs 30 on Pro)
- **Multi-user workspaces** with `owner` / `responder` / `viewer` roles
- **Group-chat alerts** — Telegram groups with per-server topics for threading
- **On-call rotation** — assign primary / secondary / escalation per shift
- **Per-seat add-ons** — $5 per extra seat above the included headcount
- **Native iOS + Android apps** with push notifications, biometric unlock for command confirmation, inline restart/silence/ack

---

## Workspace roles

| Role | What they can do |
|:-----|:-----------------|
| Owner | Manage billing, invite/remove members, full control |
| Responder | Acknowledge alerts, set silences, run remote commands |
| Viewer | Read-only — see alerts and dashboards, can't act |

Roles are workspace-scoped. A human can be in multiple workspaces with different roles in each.

---

## On-call rotation

Phase 3 introduces a simple rotation system:

- Define a **rotation** (e.g. "weekday daytime", "weekend on-call")
- Assign **shifts** (`Mon 09:00–17:00 → Alex`, `Mon 17:00–09:00 → Sam`)
- Configure **escalation** (page secondary if primary doesn't ack within 10 min)

Alerts route to whoever's on shift, with optional secondary fallback. Escalation walks up the chain until somebody acks.

---

## When Team beats Pro

- You've outgrown 10 servers.
- More than one human needs to see alerts.
- You want different humans paged at different times.
- You want a single chat where the whole team sees the day's incidents (Telegram group with topics).

---

## Phase 3 release timing

Realistic ship: **Q1 2027**. Mobile apps are the heaviest part of Phase 3 (native iOS + Android, APNs + FCM wiring, app store review). The non-mobile pieces (workspaces, rotation, group-chat) may land earlier as a "Team Lite" tier — see [the roadmap](https://github.com/stackpatrol/stackpatrol) for current status.

If you need rotation today and can't wait, the workaround is to share a single Pro account between team members and route alerts to a private Telegram group (manual setup). That's not great long-term, but it works.
